## What to Include

A useful report covers:

- a description of the vulnerability and its impact
- steps to reproduce or a proof-of-concept
- affected versions or components
- any suggested mitigations

## Response Process

1. Acknowledgment within a few days.
2. Coordinated disclosure: a fix will be prepared before public disclosure.
3. You will be credited in the release notes unless you prefer otherwise.

## Bug Bounties

We may offer bounties paid in Bitcoin for critical vulnerabilities, but cannot
make firm commitments due to budget constraints. Severity and impact determine
eligibility on a case-by-case basis.
